About Me

I took up programming as a career after working seven years in the coffee industry as a Supervisor. Being a self-taught programmer, I know learning every day is important. So that’s what I do every day; learn more. I am currently living in Colorado with my wonderful wife Ayla, and our cat, Figaro.
